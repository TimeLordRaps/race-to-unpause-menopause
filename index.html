why this exists

Make menopause legible, tractable, and eventually interruptible.

Menopause is usually framed as a passive inevitability. This site treats it as a biological state transition with measurable pathways: ovarian reserve depletion, DNA damage load, stress-axis disruption, inflammatory damage, mitochondrial failure, fibrosis, genotype-specific risk, and the feedback loops around them.

The goal is not fake certainty. The goal is a fast, updateable front page for the strongest mechanisms, the most serious targets, the known limits, and the current state of the race.

submission rule

Required before submit

Every form asks for the claim, the mechanism or measurement behind it, the evidence anchor, and the unresolved gap. That keeps the public board sortable instead of turning into supplement-ad improv.
